Food Composition Table

Food Composition Table In Mongolia, the first “Food composition table” was established and published in 1997. It contains information for the following eight groups of products. In the first FCT, a total of 12 nutrients of protein, fat, carbohydrates, fiber, ash, and vitamins A, C, E, Ca, K, Na, and Fe are covered in 288 products of the above eight groups. It was managed by the Ministry of Food, agriculture, and Light Industry. The FCT is available in hard copy. It contains few types of foods and has not been updated for more than 20 years.
